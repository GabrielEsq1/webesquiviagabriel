Design trends 2022

Design Marketing

We all made a mistake once we thought we would celebrate the end of the coronavirus in 2021. This year, we saw how the persistent pandemic disease hurt everyone. However, let's take a much closer look at how emerging trends in graphic design will shape marketing, reporting, recruiting, and even the way we communicate as humans. 

On top of that, you'll learn benchmark graphic design tips on how you can bring these trends to your presentations, reports, infographics, and social media content. 2022 is going to be a year of creative pragmatism Creative pragmatism is the common thread running through graphic design trends in 2022. 

As a graphic and communication designer who has overcome the wild roller coaster of a trip this year, I will draw a picture (this time with words and images!) of which I interpret as creative pragmatism. What is creative pragmatism? Creativity sparks the imagination, which means being bold, expressive and inspiring. 

As much, pragmatism means being realistic based on practical rather than theoretical considerations. Thus we arrive at creative pragmatism. It explains a bolder and more expressive design approach yet with a more relevant goal to communicate.
